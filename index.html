Movie Tickets & Netflix Subscription Trend

In today's world, people have been shifting from going to theaters to using streaming platforms at home.
Consumers can access their favorite shows more easily, without going to theaters or waiting for broadcasting schedule.

In the US, the number of movie tickets sold has been generally decreasing while Netflix subscriptions have been increasing.

The gap between the two are growing even more due to COVID-19 pandemic making people stay at home.

So will streaming platform continue to rise?

With various streaming platforms gaining popularity in today's world, streaming service market is set to grow more in the future.

As all our team members love using these platforms, we wanted to provide meaningful information and trends regrading this market. We hope you enjoyed exploring the streaming service market!

With the rising popularity and importance of various streaming platforms including Netflix and Disney+ , OTT market has been evidently growing and the consumers are able to access their favorite shows more easily.


These growths have so much to offer to the companies as well as the consumers such as providing emotional relief. As people who received these values ourselves, we hope to provide meaningful information and trends regarding movies on multiple streaming platforms and hope that the OTT market continues to expand and provide positive utilities to its consumers.
